GeoNames web service

Geonaming is performed with the help of Web services. Under Window>Preferences>GPS>Webservices you can select the geonaming (reverse geocoding) web service of your choice.

The GeoNames web service offers free and premium services allowing you to increase to volume of your queries:
Additional geonames.org parameters
We recommend registering with GeoNames.org for a free or a premium account. After you have registered please enter your user name into this field like this:
Example: username=myname
or this
Example: myname

If you have additional parameters separate them with '&' like this:
Example: username=myname&password=mytoken.
