Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Q: When does ZoRa Photo Director access the Internet and which informations are transmitted?

A: ZoRa Photo Director tries to keep the interaction with the Internet at a minimum. The Internet is only accessed in the following cases:

  • Showing maps (via Google Maps) 
  • Adding geographical names to geographical positions (via Geonames.org). 
  • Uploading images to an FTP account
  • Uploading images to a web community
  • Web community authorization
  • Updating web community account information (albums, categories, keywords, etc).
  • Importing categories from a web community
  • Check for Updates (on demand or automatically)
