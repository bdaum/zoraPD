Mouse preferences

The Mouse preference page allows you configuring the behavior of the mouse wheel and mouse movements.

  • Speed
    • Zoom and Pan. Changes the mouse sensitivity in the presentation views (slide, exhibition and web gallery editor), the image viewer, and the Sleeves view.
    • Wheel inertia. Determines the lag of mouse wheel movements.
  • Control keys
    • Zoom. Determines the modifier key (Shift, Alt) that switches from pan to zoom when dragging the mouse in the Sleeves view, Exhibition editor, Slideshow editor, Webgallery editor and Image viewer.
    • Mouse wheel behavior. Determines the modifier keys (Shift, Alt) to switch between scrolling and zooming in the Lightbox view, the Strip views, and the Trashcan.

Note: For best operation with a touch sensitive table or pad (e.g. Wacom) on platforms without dedicated touch support, press the button Touch Settings. This will adjust Zoom key and Mouse wheel behavior appropriately. This button is only visible on those platforms.
