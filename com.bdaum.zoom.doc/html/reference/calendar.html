Calendar view

The calendar view shows and overview over a calendar month. The month shown depends on the selelected images or the selected collection. It can be modified by using the navigation buttons in the Calendar views toolbar, the navigation items in the local pull down menu, or the cursor keys.


  • Local menu functions and local tool buttons
    You can access local menu functions by clicking the triangle symbol on the local toolbar.
    • Next month. This will advance the calendar by one month.
    • Previous month. This will step back one month.
    • Next year. This will advance the calendar by one year.
    • Previous year. This will step back one year.
  • Mouse gestures
    • Single click on the month name. Opens a gallery with all images of that month.
    • Single click on a day field. Opens a gallery with all images of that day.
    • Single click on a week field. Opens a gallery with all images of that week.
    • Swipe to the left. This will advance the calendar by one month.
    • Swipe to the right. This will step back one month.
    • Swipe towards the top. This will advance the calendar by one year.
    • Swipe towards the bottom.  This will step back one year.
  • Key actions
    • *. Opens a gallery with all images of the month shown in the calendar.
    • Number between 0 and 31. Opens a gallery with all images of that day.
    • Arrow left. This will step back one month.
    • Arrow right. This will advance the calendar by one month.
    • Arrow up. This will step back one year.
    • Arrow down. This will advance the calendar by one year.
    • Home. This will step back to January of the same year.
    • End. This will advance to December of the same year.
    • Page up. This will step back to the temporal beginning of the catalog.
    • Page down. This will advance to the temporal end of the catalog.
