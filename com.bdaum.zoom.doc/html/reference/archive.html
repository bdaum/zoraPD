Archiving

The archiving action copies the whole catalog including all image files and associated files such as voice notes and raw recipes to another destination. The chosen destination folder must be empty and must accomodate the required space.

Depending on the catalog time the copy process may last a long time during which the application cannot be used for other purposes.

The resultung archive consists from a (modified) copy of the catalog, the index files and copies of the images and associated files. The original folder structure is NOT maintained. Instead images may be renamed in case of name clashes. On demand, the archived catalog and the archived files can be write-protected.
