Find Series

This function is used to find time series within the catalog. Several options allow fine-tuning this search:

  • Series type: Choose from the following series types:
    • All. No specific type. All series satisfying the criteria explained below are shown.
    • Exposure bracket. Series with variying exposure (light value) but constant subject distance and focal length.
    • Focus bracket. Series with variying subject distance but constant exposure and focal length. Note that this series type does not work for many cameras (especially compact camera) as these camera don't deliver the necessary EXIF data.
    • Zoom bracket. Series with varying focal length but constant exposure and subject distance.
    • Rapid fire. Series with an interval of less than 250 msec between shots.
    • Other. Other series NOT falling into the above categories.
  • Maximum interval: The maximum time interval (in seconds) between two consecutive images. A time difference that is larger than the specified value is regarded as a break in the series.
  • Minimum length: Series that are shorter than the specified length are not shown. 
  • Separate by file format: Typically, images of a series shot with a digital camera have the same file format. This option helps to narrow down the search to genuine series.
  • Ignore derived images: This option restricts the search to original images by excluding derived images such as the results of RAW-to-DNG conversions or RAW development processes.
  • Find within current collection: If checked, the search is restricted to the current collection. If not, the whole catalog is searched.
