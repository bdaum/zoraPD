Perspectives

Perspectives define the initial set and layout of views in the Workbench window. They provide a set of functionality aimed at accomplishing a specific type of task.

Opening perspectives

To open a new perspective:

  1. Click the Open Perspective button on the Perspective Bar on the right side of the Workbench window. (This provides the same function as the Window>Perspective...Open Perspective... menu function.)
 
  1. Select the perspective that you want to open.

Switching between perspectives

Open perspectives are represented by icons on the perspective bar. When you have more than one perspective open, you can switch between them by clicking the icons on the perspective bar.

Changing the layout of perspectives

You can easily change the layout of perspectives:

  • Modify the size of views by dragging the sashes between views.
  • Move views to  a different location by dragging them by their tab.
  • Minimize a view by clicking the Minimize button in the views headline. You can restore the view with a right click and selecting Restore.
  • Close view with a click on their close button in their tab.
  • Open new views with the Window>Show View sub menu.
In addition, you may configure the menu and toolbar items shown in each perspective via menu function Window>Perspective...>Configure Perspective....

If you want to return to the initial layout of a perspective after such operations invoke Window>Perspective...>Reset Perspective.

Saving a user defined perspective

If you have modified a perspective, you can save your changes for future use.
  1. Switch to the perspective that you want to save.
  2. Click Window>Perspective...>Save Perspective As....
  3. Type a new name for the perspective into the Name field.
  4. Click OK.

The name of the new perspective is added to the Window>Perspective...>Open Perspective... menu.

Deleting a user defined perspective

You can delete perspectives that you defined yourself:

  1. Invoke the Window>Preferences>Perspectives*.
  2. Select the perspective you want to delete and press Delete.
  3. Click OK.
