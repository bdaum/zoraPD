Slide properties

The slide properties dialog allows editing or defining several properties of an individual slide such as:
  • Title
  • Initial delay before the Fade-in process begins in seconds
  • Fade-in duration in seconds
  • Effects during Fade-in
  • Zoom amount (0:100) and direction (-100:100, -100:100). A zoom amount of 100% will cause a  zoom-in by factor 2.
  • Duration in seconds. The standard duration for normal slides is the duration defined in the slideshow. For title slides, it is the maximum of that time and the time calculated from the text length, as configured in Preferences> Hover.
  • Fade-out duration in seconds
  • Suppressing the attached voice note
