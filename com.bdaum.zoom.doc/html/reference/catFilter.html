Catalog filter


The catalog filter is used to hide unused or unwanted catalog items:

  • Empty person albums. Hides all empty person albums. This can be useful when albums from Google Picasa are imported, that do not contain images also present in the catalog.
  • Person albums. Hides the whole group of Person Albums
  • Categories. Hides the whole group of Category collections.
  • Ratings. Hides the whole group of Rating collections.
  • Directories. Hides the whole group of Directory collections.
  • Timeline. Hides the whole group of Timeline collections.
  • Locations. Hides the whole group of Location collections.
  • Past imports. Hides all Import collections except Last Import.

In addition, it can be used to show only items with the selected color code.
