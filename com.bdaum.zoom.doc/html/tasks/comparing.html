Comparing images

To compare two images side by side, select both images in a gallery. Then press TAB or F4, or use the context menu to invoke the internal image viewer (external image viewers are not used for comparing images). The images are shown side by side. On each image you may apply the mouse gestures or keyboard actions for controlling pan and zoom, as described for the internal image viewer. By pressing F4 you can switch to synchronized mode where each zoom or pan action is simultanously applied to both images. To switch back to the unsynchronized mode, press F4 again.
