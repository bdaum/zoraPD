Video

ZoRa PhotoDirector uses the VLC video player (VideoLAN client) as an internal video engine.
VLC is not included in the ZoRa PhotoDirector distribution. In many cases you will already have a full VLC installation on your computer. Otherwise you must download and install it.
Finally, you must tell ZoRa PhotoDirector where the VLC executable is found.

VLC

  • VLC executable location. Please specify the location of the VLC executable (vlc.exe under Windows). Usually this is found in your program folder in subfolder VideoLAN\VLC\.
