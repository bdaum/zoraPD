Internal video viewer

The internal video viewer can be invoked by pressing a modifier key and double-clicking the video thumbnail (or via the context menu). The following modifier keys are possible:


Shift Forces the internal viewer.
Alt Forces the internal viewer being invoked in black&white mode*.

*) If the viewer is called while black&white mode is already active, it switches to color mode, instead.

On invocation the video viewer will immediately show an enlarged thumbnail while loading the video.

The following mouse and key actions are available:

  • Move the mouse to pause the video viewer and to show the control panel.
  • Press ESC to leave the video viewer. Alternatively, you can click the STOP button in the control panel.
