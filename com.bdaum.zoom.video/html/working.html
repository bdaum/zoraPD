Working with videos

Videos can be imported in the same way as photos. During the import process the very first frame will be used as the catalog thumbnail.

In some cases this may not be satisfactory, for example if the video starts with black frames. In such a case you may want to determine the catalog thumbnail manually. To do so Shift+double click the video thumbnail to invoke the internal video viewer. Within the viewer position to a frame of your choice, then click the camera icon for a snapshot. Done.
