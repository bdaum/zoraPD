PDF wizard

The PDF function is invoked via menu function File>Export...>Others>PDF It is used to generate a PDF document from the selected images..

You may either export selected images or export all images of a slide show, exhibition, or web gallery selected in the Catalog View.

You may change the proposed layout and the image quality. Print quality PDFs have a higher resolution (300 dpi) than Screen quality PDFs (96 dpi). They are, of course, also larger in size. 

You can in addition influence the Output Quality with these setttings.

The layout options follow closely the Print dialog.
