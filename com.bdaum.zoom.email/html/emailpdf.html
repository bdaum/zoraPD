Email as PDF wizard

The Email as PDF function is invoked via menu function File>Export...>Email>PDF. It is used to transmit the selected images to the platform's email client embedded into a PDF document.

You may either export selected images or export all images of a slide show, exhibition, or web gallery selected in the Catalog View.

You may change the proposed layout and the image quality. Print quality PDFs have a higher resolution (300 dpi) than Screen quality PDFs (96 dpi). They are, of course, also larger in size and transmit slower.

You can in addition influence the Output Quality with these setttings.

The layout options follows closely the Print dialog.

You may also add a subject and a message. The mail must still be completed with the receivers email address but this will be done in the email client.

The following options are available for the export operation:
  • Optionally, you may add watermarks for the protection of you intellectual property to images (not when uploading originals).
  • You can track the export. Later, you may view these records in the Properties view under  ZoRa Photo Director>Track.
  • You can filter images and send only public or public+medium images.
