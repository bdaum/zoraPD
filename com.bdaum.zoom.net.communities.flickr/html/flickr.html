Flickr accounts

Access to Flickr accounts is by explicit authorization. The first time when accessing the service you will be asked to manually log-in into your accout. Flickr will show you the ZoRa Photo Director is asking for permission to access your site. By granting access you will enable ZoRa to upload images. If by any reason ZoRa Photo Director should loose this permission, you will be asked again to grant permission.

Flickr accounts
  • enforce size and transmission limits that are shown on page Capacity of the account dialog.
  • allow an image being placed in multiple albums similar to what is possible in the ZoRa Photo Director.
  • support geocoding and tagging
  • do not support categories
  • Only professional accounts allow for the replacement of images.
    When uploading an image twice on a free acount, two copies will be stored in your flickr-gallery. You may then want to delete the older copy manually.
