Locr accounts

Access to Locr accounts is by password. It is therefore necessary to enter the password into the account properties dialog. If you change the password at a later time, you must also update the account properties.

Locr accounts
  • free Locr accounts enforce limit the available bandwidth and the number of albums. These restrictions are shown on page Capacity of the account dialog.
  • allow an image being placed in multiple albums similar to what is possible in the ZoRa Photo Director.
  • support geocoding and tagging
  • do not support categories
  • do not support the replacement of images. When uploading an image twice, two copies will be stored in your Locr-gallery. You may then want to delete the older copy manually.
