Photocommunities

Photocommunities are a popular option to publish images on the Internet. Before being able to upload images to such a community you must create an account with them and enter the account details into ZoRa. 

The amount and maximum size depends on the particular community. Usually, free communities tend to limit the amout and size of images more than commercial communities. Communities also differ in their support for albums (photosets) and categories and the method of access authorization. Please refer to the help pages of a particular community for details.
