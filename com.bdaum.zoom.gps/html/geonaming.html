Geo-naming

Latitude and Longitude are for human readers not really speaking. To relate the geographical coordinates with place names, geo-naming services on the Internet is used. The resulting place names are stored in the IPTC meta data under Location created (the camera position). The place names are added to the keywords, too. However, this behavior can be disabled.  (Window>Preferences>GPS*)

Geonaming happens automatically during geo-tagging. It is possible to geo-name images that are already geo-tagged, too. (Metadata>Geoname images)

Note: For geo-naming we use the services of www.geonames.org and Google (recommended).These services are free but the number of requests per day is limited. The limit is liberal enough for the purposes of single photographers. However, if you require higher traffic rates and higher availability of the service, feel free to enter a commercial arrangement with one or both of the providers. Provider selection and entry of you commercial credentials is done under Window>Preferences>GPS>Webservices*.

*) On Mac use ZoRa Photo Director>Preferences>...
