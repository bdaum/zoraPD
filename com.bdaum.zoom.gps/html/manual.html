Manual Geotagging

Geo-tags can be applied manually. Select the images in question, then switch to the Map view. Click on the leftmost pin in the top region, then follow the instructions. You may apply this operation to a single image or to several images in one step.

When working with a single image you may also define the image direction (the direction in which the camera was turned) by clicking onto the direction pin.

If an image already is geo-tagged, its location is displayed as a marker. You can change this location by dragging the marker into a different location. Alternatetively, you may also set a new marker by clicking on the leftmost pin in the top region. This will, however, change the position of ALL selected images.
