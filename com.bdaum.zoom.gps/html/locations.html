Locations

The IPTC standard defines different locations that can be defined for an image:

  • Location created (IPTC>Administration>Location created) indicates the camera position when the image was taken. The location entries are created automatically when geo-tagging images. 
  • Location shown (IPTC>Origin>Location created) can contain several locations displayed in the image. These locations are usually created manually.
Each location consists of hierarchical place names (world region, country, state, city, sub location) and/or coordinates. ZoRa Photo Director maintains a list of already defined locations for quick selection. New locations may be defined by pressing the Add... button and entering their details or by locating them on the map. To do so, first press the Map... button, then click on the green marker in the top region of the Map dialog, and then on the location in the map.

Locations can be viewed and edited through the menu function Catalog>Properties>Locations.
