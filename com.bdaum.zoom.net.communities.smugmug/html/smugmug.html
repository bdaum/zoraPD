SmugMug accounts

Access to SmugMug accounts is by password. It is therefore necessary to enter the password into the account properties dialog. If you change the password at a later time, you must also update the account properties.

SmugMug accounts
  • SmugMug limits only the maximum size of images. These restrictions are shown on page Capacity of the account dialog.
  • allow an image being placed only into a single album. When uploading images it is therefore necessary to select the target album.
  • support geocoding and tagging
  • support categories and subcategories
  • support the replacement of images.
