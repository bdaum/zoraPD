AI Preferences

Artificial intelligence services are used in ZoRa PhotoDirector for image recognition. In particular these service are used in the Categorize... function for proposing categories, keywords, and optionally privacy levels, image descriptions, and face regions.

The AI preference page collects the currently implemented AI preference services within a tabbed folder. A checkbox at the top of the page allows you to switch these services on or off altogether.

Some services deliver their results in English only. To obtain the results in a language of your choice, you must activate the translation service. ZoRa PhotoDirector integrates the Microsoft translation service. To activate it, open an account at Microsoft Azure and register for the Translate Text service. Then enter the obtained Key1 or Key2 into the preference page and select an output language.

At the moment of writing Microsoft provides 10000 translation requests per month for free (Categories and keywords count as one request, descriptions as another). Higher volumes need a payed account.
