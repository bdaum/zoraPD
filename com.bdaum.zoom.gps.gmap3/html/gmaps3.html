Google Maps

Google Maps 3 is the official Google Maps API. Here, we use it's Road view, Aerial (satellite) view, Hybrid view, and Terrain view.

Minimaps are not supported but Street view is supported via the pegman at the top of the zoom control.

Google Maps 3 requires registration with Google and the generation of an API-key. This key can be entered under Window>Preferences>GPS>Webservices .
